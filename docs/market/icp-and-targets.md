## Buyer Persona

**Primary:** Compliance Manager / GRC Lead
- Solo or small team (1-3 people)
- Manages ISO 27001 + SOC 2 (sometimes GDPR, HIPAA)
- Spends 15+ hours/week on documentation and evidence
- Frustrated with existing tools ("expensive checkbox software")
- Reports to CTO or VP Engineering

**Secondary:** CTO at companies without a dedicated compliance person
- Compliance is "one of 12 things I do"
- Would love to delegate to a tool
- Price sensitive — current tools feel expensive for what they do
