# Competitive Landscape

**Last Updated:** 2026-03-21
**Status:** Active
**Role:** cascade-target

**TL;DR:** Three main competitors in AI compliance automation: Drata ($200M+ ARR, recently raised pricing), Vanta (strong in SOC 2, weaker on ISO), SecureFrame (smallest, best UX). All focus on certification management, not evidence quality. None automate the compliance manager's daily documentation work — the gap CompliBot targets.

## Key Intel

### Drata
> **[UPDATE 2026-03-21 · Source: pricing page]** New enterprise tier at $60K/year. Mid-market tier unchanged at $15K.

- Market leader by revenue. Strong brand, weak product depth.
- Practitioners call it "a glorified spreadsheet." `Confidence: Soft signal` (2 online reviews)
- Integration count is the moat — 75+ native integrations.

### Vanta
- Strongest on SOC 2. ISO 27001 support is newer and less mature.
- Best auto-monitoring for cloud infrastructure.
- Weakest on evidence packaging — exports require manual cleanup.

### SecureFrame
- Smallest player, best UX.
- Founded by ex-Googlers. Strong engineering culture.
- Most likely to build AI-native features, but resource-constrained.

---

## The Gap

All three platforms focus on **getting certified** — managing the audit process, tracking controls, scheduling reviews. None of them address the daily work of keeping compliance documentation current, accurate, and audit-ready between certification cycles.

Practitioners report spending 60% of their time on evidence collection and documentation, not on actual compliance decisions. (→ external-insights.md#session-2-key-insights)
